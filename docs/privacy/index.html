Live Transcriber
Privacy Policy

Effective July 14, 2026 · 中文版本见下方

Live Transcriber is local-first. Recordings and transcripts stay in the app's private storage on your device by default. We do not operate a backend that receives your recordings.

Data stored on your device

Recordings, transcripts, titles, summaries, tags, settings, and related metadata are stored in the app's private container on your device by default. This content remains there until you delete it or remove the app.

Microphone and speech recognition

Microphone access is used to record audio. Speech Recognition access is used to convert speech into text. The default live transcription workflow uses Apple's system Speech frameworks. Live Transcriber does not send live microphone audio to a developer-operated server.

Optional iCloud sync

If you enable iCloud sync, recordings and their index may be stored in the app-private iCloud container and CloudKit private database associated with your Apple Account. Apple provides and governs these services. You can disable iCloud sync in the app's Settings.

Optional local model downloads

You may choose to download Whisper or Qwen3 model files from third-party model hosting services. Downloaded models are stored on your device and can be deleted in Settings. When you select local processing, transcription and summarization run on your device.

Optional OpenAI transcription

OpenAI transcription is disabled by default. If you enable it, enter your own OpenAI API key, and manually choose OpenAI transcription for a saved recording, that selected audio is sent directly from your device to OpenAI. Your API key is stored in the iOS Keychain. OpenAI processes the request under its own terms and privacy policy. Do not use this option for content you do not want to send to OpenAI.

Location and Reminders

Location is used only when you choose to attach a location to a saved recording. Reminders access is used only when you choose to export an action item to Apple Reminders. We do not send this information to a developer-operated server.

Analytics, advertising, and tracking

Live Transcriber does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites.

Retention and deletion

You can delete individual recordings and downloaded models in the app. Removing the app deletes its local private data. Content synchronized through iCloud is managed through your Apple Account and Apple's services. Data sent to an optional third-party service is subject to that service's retention policy.

Children's privacy

Live Transcriber is a general-purpose productivity tool and is not directed specifically to children. We do not knowingly collect children's personal information through a developer-operated service.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the app's features or data practices change. The effective date above identifies the latest version.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, open an issue in the Live Transcriber GitHub repository or contact @livetranscriber on Telegram.

Live Transcriber
隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 14 日

Live Transcriber 以本地处理为优先。录音和转录内容默认保存在设备上的 App 私有空间中。我们不运营接收用户录音的开发者后端。

保存在设备上的数据

录音、转录文本、标题、摘要、标签、设置及相关元数据默认保存在设备上的 App 私有容器中。这些内容会一直保留，直到你主动删除或卸载 App。

麦克风与语音识别

麦克风权限用于录制音频，语音识别权限用于把语音转换成文字。默认实时转录流程使用 Apple 系统语音框架。Live Transcriber 不会把实时麦克风音频发送到开发者运营的服务器。

可选 iCloud 同步

如果你启用 iCloud 同步，录音文件和索引可能保存在与你的 Apple 账户关联的 App 私有 iCloud 容器和 CloudKit 私有数据库中。这些服务由 Apple 提供和管理。你可以在 App 设置中关闭 iCloud 同步。

可选本地模型下载

你可以选择从第三方模型托管服务下载 Whisper 或 Qwen3 模型。模型保存在设备上，并可在设置中删除。选择本地处理时，转录和摘要在设备上运行。

可选 OpenAI 转录

OpenAI 转录默认关闭。只有当你启用该功能、输入自己的 OpenAI API Key，并为某个已保存录音手动选择 OpenAI 转录时，所选音频才会从设备直接发送到 OpenAI。API Key 保存在 iOS 钥匙串中。OpenAI 将依据其自身条款和隐私政策处理请求。请勿使用该选项发送你不希望交由 OpenAI 处理的内容。

位置与提醒事项

仅当你选择给录音附加位置时，App 才会使用位置权限。仅当你选择把行动项导出到 Apple 提醒事项时，App 才会使用提醒事项权限。我们不会把这些信息发送到开发者运营的服务器。

分析、广告与追踪

Live Transcriber 不包含第三方广告或分析 SDK，也不会跨 App 或网站追踪用户。

保留与删除

你可以在 App 中删除录音和已下载模型。卸载 App 会删除其本机私有数据。通过 iCloud 同步的内容由你的 Apple 账户和 Apple 服务管理。发送给可选第三方服务的数据受该服务的数据保留政策约束。

儿童隐私

Live Transcriber 是通用效率工具，并非专门面向儿童。我们不通过开发者运营的服务故意收集儿童个人信息。

政策更新

当 App 功能或数据处理方式发生变化时，本政策可能更新。以上生效日期表示当前版本。

联系方式

如有隐私或支持问题，请在 Live Transcriber GitHub 仓库提交 Issue，或通过 Telegram 联系 @livetranscriber。
