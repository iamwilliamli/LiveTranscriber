# Code of Conduct

LiveTranscriber is a small project, but contributions and discussions should
still be clear, respectful, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Keep discussion focused on the project, code, documentation, and user impact.
- Give technical feedback with enough context that someone can act on it.
- Respect privacy. Do not post someone else's recordings, transcripts, account
  details, or private information.
- Disclose conflicts of interest when relevant, especially around commercial
  forks or derivative apps.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Trolling, repeated off-topic posting, or bad-faith argument.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Pressuring maintainers or contributors for unpaid work on a specific timeline.
- Misrepresenting the project's license, authorship, or commercial attribution
  requirements.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, or delete comments; close or lock issues and pull
requests; reject contributions; or block participants who violate this code.

For sensitive security or privacy concerns, follow `SECURITY.md` instead of
posting details publicly.
